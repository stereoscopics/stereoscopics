About

by Paulo A.
published on Friday, July 2, 2021

Hey, here is the (wanna be) photographer that started this site. I am a very beginner that wants to grow in this hobby. To preserve memories is something I wish more people would consider as well.

This a simple place where I share the shots I judged were good and I would like to revisit in the future.

The name is from the stereoscopic effect, when you combine two-dimensional images in the brain to give the perception of 3D depth, although most pictures here will be '2D flat' I hope they can carry 'depth', that they can make you perceive something beside the 2D dimension.

My gear is pretty simple, but as soon as I get more into photography I will get more, for the moment this is what I use:

Cameras:

  • Canon PowerShot SX20 IS
  • Canon 600D (also named: EOS Rebel T3i / EOS Kiss X5)

Lenses:

  • Canon EF-S 18-55mm f/ 3.5-5.6 IS
  • Pentacon 50 mm f/ 1.8-16 multi coating auto Lens
  • Pentor 135 mm f/ 2.8-22 Auto Lens
  • Revuenon 135 mm f/ 2.8-16 Auto multi coated Lens
  • Vivitar 70-210 mm f/ 4.5-22 MC Macro Lens
  • Vivitar MC 80-200 mm f/ 4.5-32 Zoom Lens
